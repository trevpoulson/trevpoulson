Blizzard Shop

Problem:

Update the Blizzard Digital Shop to account for our growing product catalog, handle localization and regionality for our global offerings, and work with all device types and sizes.

Solution:

Redesign the Blizzard Shop following mobile-first responsive design patterns in a way that allows for scalability and personalization (by locale, region, and individual). Additionally, we wanted to address usability issues uncovered during user-research and provide additional features to help our users find the products that are right for them. All of this needed to be done in a way that showcased Blizzard's franchises and overall brand.

Overview:

Initially, I was approached about redesigning the shop to make it more scalable for future releases and make the designs more responsive. Through user-research and testing, we were able to find and address usability issues as well as problems our users were having finding the right products.

In the end we were able to address the business needs of scalability, responsiveness, & personalization and users needs with better categorization and discoverability, while demonstrating the level of polish our users expect.

Since the launch in late 2017, we continue to monitor our performance though established KPIs and continuous user research iterating where needed.
